Combinational Circuits
A combinational circuit, also called a combinational logic circuit, is a digital electronic circuit whose output is determined by present inputs only. The output of a combinational logic circuit does not depend on the history of the circuit operation. In other words, a combinational circuit is a digital logic circuit whose output depends only on the present input values and does not depend on any feedback or previous input or output values.
